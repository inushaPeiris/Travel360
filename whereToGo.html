AN ISLAND FULL OF PHENOMENAL WONDERS THAT WILL CAPTIVATE YOUR HEARTS

A Tropical island located in the Indian ocean, bustling with a vast level of diversity and compactness. This magnificent teardrop-shaped isle has a recorded history of over 2500 years and is now home to 8 of UNESCO’s world heritage sites. The island in all its glory offers - beautiful cultural & natural treasure, breathtaking landscape, ancient heritage sites, fun-filled excursions, adventurous events & authentic cuisine.
